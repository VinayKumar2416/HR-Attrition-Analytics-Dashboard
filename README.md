# 📖 About the Project

Employee attrition is one of the biggest challenges faced by organizations, directly impacting productivity, recruitment costs, and employee morale. This project analyzes HR data to identify workforce trends, measure attrition, and provide meaningful insights that support strategic HR decision-making.

The project demonstrates a complete analytics workflow using **Python** for data preparation, **SQL** for business analysis, and **Power BI** for creating an interactive dashboard.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across the organization.
- Monitor important HR KPIs.
- Identify departments and job roles with higher turnover.
- Study the relationship between overtime, satisfaction, and attrition.
- Build an interactive dashboard for HR managers.

---

# 💼 Business Scenario

Organizations often struggle to understand **why employees leave** and **which workforce segments require immediate attention**.

By analyzing employee demographics, compensation, satisfaction, experience, and departmental information, this project helps HR teams identify patterns and make informed decisions to improve employee retention.

---

# 📈 Major Findings

- Research & Development recorded the highest employee attrition.
- Laboratory Technicians experienced the highest turnover among job roles.
- Employees with **0–1 years** at the company showed the highest attrition.
- The **26–35 age group** contributed the largest share of employee turnover.
- Employees working overtime were more likely to leave the organization.
